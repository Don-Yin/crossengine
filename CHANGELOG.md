- initial release
- multi-asset backtesting engine with proportional commission and slippage models
- STAY signal for partial rebalancing (freeze share count, let weight drift)
- limit/stop order support
- cross-engine concordance testing API (ours, bt, vectorbt, backtrader, cvxportfolio)
- 15 benchmark strategies across 30 stratified asset buckets (180 S&P 500 stocks)
- 44 tests including full 5-engine concordance validation
- JOSS paper submission
